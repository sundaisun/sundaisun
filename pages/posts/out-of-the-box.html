Overview

Out of the Box (OOTB) is a fun and exciting, all-in-one smart solution designed to help you to have a better advance movie screening experience. Reserve screening tickets, load some coupons, check lining status, and enjoy the movie. OOTB is the one of a kind convenient survival kit for movie lovers.

Why Out of the Box?

Advance movie screening is an economical and fun way to watch movies before they premiere. However, the screenings are always overbooked, the process of getting a ticket is complicated and not eco-friendly. Think out of the box, use OOTB to make screening experience hassle-free.

Strategies

Streamlined process.

Filter your screenings by time, location or popularity, watch a trailer and RSVP. No more copying and pasting promo codes, no more forced twitter posts, OOTB handles everything else automatically.

Save more on free.

Enjoy snacks and drinks at the theater for a lower price. Every time you review the movie after its screening, OOTB loads up credits/coupons into your card, so you can use them for your next viewing.

Promote more movies.

OOTB creates a screening ecosystem. Users will be encouraged to share a review on social media in order to win prizes and coupons, thus increasing your movie's exposure through multiple channels.

Deliverables

Brand identity

The OOTB character represents the product's image: fun, witty and bold. This character is a repeated theme on the website and app menu. The name "Out of the box" has double meanings: 1. no more waiting in lines. Use our product to stay out of the box office; 2. think outside the box, use our product instead.

Responsive website

The website is built with self defined framework using SCSS. Header autoplays movie trailer in the background on mute, it transitions into static movie poster when on medium and small screens.

Mobile app

The application lets you quickly select screening location and time, then register with one simple tap. Given the consideration of large smartphone screens, the menu bar sits on bottom of the app, to be easily accessed by swipe and tap.
