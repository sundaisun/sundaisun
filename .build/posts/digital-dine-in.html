User Experience | Interaction | Visual Design

Project Goal

To design a seamless and enjoyable dine-in experience at McDonald’s, reduce waiting time in queue, and to inform customers regarding health facts of food at McDonald’s.

Challenges

  • Too many options-they're slowing me down;
  • Cannot just get what I always get;
  • I want to switch food items.
  • Waiting is too boring.

Idea sketches


Solutions

Personalized menu.

New menu is cleanly categorized and presented with "cards". When user interacts with the card, it expands, showing meal content, type and price. User may have the option to view his / her order history and most frequently ordered items.

Customize your meal.

Efficiency is key. The ordering app limits number of items you can swap in the customization section, so that people don't end up spending too much time deciding what to eat. Price is updated in real time so you are always informed.

Easy Payment.

No more standing in line. Simply pay within the app.

Waiting is fun.

The app tells you an estimated wait so you can coordinate your time better. Play a leisure game, or even order a song to play at the restaurant.
